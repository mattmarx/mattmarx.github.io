Matt Marx is the Bruce F. Failing, Sr. Chair in Entrepreneurship at the Cornell SC Johnson College of Business, where he is the inaugural Faculty Director of Entrepreneurship@Cornell. He serves as a Research Associate at the National Bureau of Economic Research (NBER), Head of the Innovation Information Initiative (I3), curator of several open datasets at relianceonscience.org, and a Department Editor for Innovation & Entrepreneurship at Management Science. He was formerly and executive and inventor at two startup-companies, where he obtained six patents. See full bio here.
